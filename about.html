Our Story

Engineering Excellence At Scale

Founded in 2009 in Riyadh, CISCO PATNER has grown from a regional telecom contractor into a full-service enterprise technology company operating across 12 countries. Our 200+ engineer team has delivered some of the region's most complex digital infrastructure projects.

We believe technology should be an enabler — not a barrier. That's why we take a consultative approach to every engagement, understanding each client's unique business context before recommending solutions.

Our multidisciplinary expertise spans telecom networks, data centers, cybersecurity, ELV systems, MEP engineering, and custom software — making CISCO PATNER a one-stop partner for enterprise digital transformation.
