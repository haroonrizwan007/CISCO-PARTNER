Data Center Solutions

Enterprise-Grade Service Delivery

CISCO PATNER designs and builds world-class data centers — from enterprise server rooms to hyperscale facilities. Our end-to-end capability spans site selection, civil construction, MEP systems, IT infrastructure, network design, and ongoing managed operations.

We have delivered Tier II, Tier III, and Tier IV data centers for banking, government, and telecom clients. Our managed services team operates 24/7 NOC and SOC functions to ensure maximum availability and security.

  • Proven Tier III/IV design and construction capability
  • Reduced PUE through advanced cooling design
  • Integrated IT and facilities management
  • Scalable from 10kW to 20MW+
