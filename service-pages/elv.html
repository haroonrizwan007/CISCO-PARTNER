ELV Solutions

Enterprise-Grade Service Delivery

Our ELV division delivers fully integrated Extra Low Voltage systems for commercial towers, campuses, airports, hospitals, and government facilities. We design, supply, install, test, and commission complete ELV solutions aligned with international standards.

From structured cabling and IP-based CCTV to enterprise-grade access control and intelligent building management systems — CISCO PATNER ensures your facility is secure, connected, and efficiently managed.

  • Single contractor accountability for all ELV systems
  • Pre-tested integrated systems reduce installation time
  • Compliant with IEC, NFPA, and local authority standards
  • Ongoing maintenance contracts available
